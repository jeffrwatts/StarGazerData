Caldwell 104, also known as the Sombrero Galaxy or M104, is a spiral galaxy in Virgo, famous for its bright nucleus, large central bulge, and prominent dust lane. The galaxy resembles a sombrero hat, hence its name. It's visible in small telescopes as an elongated feature with a bright core. Larger telescopes, particularly over 8 inches, can reveal the dark dust lane that bisects the galaxy. A magnification of 100x to 150x is ideal for observing the details of the Sombrero Galaxy, especially the contrast between its bright core and the dark lane.
