Caldwell 64, the Black Eye Galaxy or M64, is a spiral galaxy in Coma Berenices, known for its dark dust lane that gives it a 'black eye' appearance. The galaxy's bright nucleus and the dark lane are visible in medium to large telescopes. Under dark skies, a magnification of 100x to 150x can reveal the contrast between the galaxy's bright core and the dark dust lane, highlighting its unique features. Caldwell 64 is an excellent target for intermediate amateur astronomers looking to observe finer details in galaxies.
