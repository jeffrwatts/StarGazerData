M17, also known as the Omega Nebula or the Swan Nebula, is an emission nebula in Sagittarius. It's visible in binoculars as a small, hazy patch, but telescopes reveal its distinctive shape, resembling a swan or the Greek letter Omega. The nebula is bright and can be observed with low to medium magnifications, around 40x to 80x. An O-III or UHC filter can greatly enhance the visibility of the nebula's structure and contrast.
