M45, commonly known as the Pleiades or the Seven Sisters, is an open star cluster in Taurus. This cluster is one of the nearest and most obvious to the naked eye in the night sky. Through binoculars or a small telescope, the stunning arrangement of its brightest stars is visible, along with a delicate nebulosity surrounding these stars. The Pleiades are best viewed at low magnification, around 20x to 40x, which allows the entire cluster to fit within the field of view, showcasing its impressive array of stars against the dark sky.
